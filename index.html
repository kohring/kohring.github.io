News and Projects

Recent highlights and an overview of past projects

Attending: International Conference on Machine Learning

At the 40th ICML in Honolulu, I had the pleasure of presenting our research paper on an efficient gradient estimation technique for learning optimal strategies in markets.

July 2023

Workshop: Deep Reinforcement Learning for Dynamic Decision Problems in Logistics and Health Care

During this workshop in Heilbronn, Stefan Heidekrüger and I presented our research results on equilibrium learning in markets.

September 2022

Event: Discussing Societal Impacts of Digitalization

Several workshops and plenary sessions discussed different aspects of the impact of digitalization on modern societies during this event called upgrade:digitalpolitik. As one aspect of this, I organized a workshop on AI and algorithms and discussed possible regulatory measures, such as the EU AI Act, with the participants.

September 2022

Release of Deep Reinforcement Learning Framework

We have released our framework for computing market equilibria via methods of multi-agent deep reinforcement learning called BNELearn.

June 2022

Machine Learning for Equilibrium Computation

Our research on equilibrium computation has been published in the Nature Machine Intelligence journal. Check out the university's press release or this tweet.

August 2021

Financial Modelling Using Online Machine Learning

The difficulty of predicting developments in the financial markets is to build a machine learning model which can adapt as quickly as possible while not neglecting learned dependencies. The project focused on online deep learning and a random forest modeling approach.

Aug 2019

Master's Thesis: Statistical Analysis of Deep Learning Prediction Models Using Bayesian Methods

This work analyzed and modified multiple deep learning approaches for solving regression problems while incorporating uncertainty.

Apr 2019

Summer School: Data Science

In this two week class, multiple aspects and the interdisciplinary nature of data science were studied in a cooperation of the University of Tokyo and the University of California, Berkeley.

Jul 2018

Term Paper: TensorFlow

In this work, a broad introduction to the machine learning framework TensorFlow by Google was given, and the usage was demonstrated on one real-world application.

Jun 2018

Seminar Talk: Dropout in Neural Networks

Dropout is a type of regularization in deep learning systems for reducing overfitting by preventing over-complex co-adaptations of neurons on training data.

Jul 2017

Bachelor's Thesis: Parareal Algorithm

The Parareal Algorithm is used for solving initial-value differential equations parallelized over multiple threads. Parareal Algorithm on Wikipedia.

Aug 2016

Seminar Talk: PageRank Algorithm

In this talk, an inner-outer method for solving a large and sparse linear system of equations, such as those occurring in a search-engine problem, is presented.

Oct 2015
